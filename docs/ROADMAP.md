## Kabus Marketplace Script Roadmap

This document outlines the planned features, improvements, and milestones for the Kabus Marketplace Script built with Laravel 11 and PHP 8.3.

On January 1st 2025, Sukunetsiz created the Kabus Marketplace Script, marking the beginning of its development. The first stable release (v1.0) was scheduled for April 4th 2025, and included the core features needed to make it a functional and reliable marketplace.

Around December 31st 2025 the creator of Kabus Marketplace Script (Sukunetsiz) vanished from the internet along with the Github repository that hosted Kabus.

I am AnonymousUser9183 and I will be carrying on the mission and goals of Sukunetsiz by continuing the development and maintenance of Kabus Marketplace Script. Upcoming milestones are listed below.
